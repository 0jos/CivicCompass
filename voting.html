Who Can Register to Vote?

  • U.S. citizens
  • Residents of the state where you're registering
  • At least 18 years old by Election Day

How to Register

  • Online: Visit your state's election website
  • By Mail: Download and mail a voter registration form
  • In Person: Visit your local election office or DMV

Ways to Vote

  • In-Person: Vote at your polling place on Election Day
  • Early Voting: Many states offer early voting before Election Day
  • Mail-In/Absentee: Request a ballot to vote by mail

Your Rights at the Polls

  • You can vote if you're in line before polls close
  • You can request assistance if you need help
  • You can request a provisional ballot if there are issues
  • You can report problems to election officials
