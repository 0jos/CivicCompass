Tips for Effective Contact

Phone Calls

  • Be brief and specific. State your name, where you live, and the issue.
  • Ask for a position. "Does the Representative support [issue]?"
  • Share your story. Personal stories are powerful.
  • Be polite. Staff members report to the official.

Sample Phone Script

"Hello, my name is [Name] and I'm a constituent from [City/ZIP]. I'm calling to urge [Senator/Representative Name] to [support/oppose] [specific bill]. This is important because [reason]. Thank you."

Writing Letters or Emails

  • Include your address. This confirms you're a constituent.
  • Focus on one issue. Don't cover multiple topics.
  • Be specific. Reference bill numbers if applicable.
  • Request a response. Ask for their position.
