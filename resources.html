Emergency Legal Hotlines

If you or someone you know needs immediate legal assistance:

  • RAICES: 1-800-354-9365 - Free legal services for immigrant families
  • National Immigrant Justice Center: (212) 629-6335
  • ACLU Immigrants' Rights: (212) 549-1200

Community Organizations

  • OLA of Eastern Long Island: (631) 209-5188 - Advocacy and services for immigrant community
  • Museum of Chinese in America: (212) 619-9785 - Cultural preservation and community support

Additional Resources

  • Know Your Rights workshops - Many community organizations offer free workshops
  • Language assistance - Most legal aid organizations can provide interpreters
  • Family preparedness - Create an emergency plan with your family
